Our Mission

To democratize access to credit in Kenya by providing fast, fair, and transparent lending solutions that empower individuals and small businesses to achieve their financial goals.

We believe that everyone deserves access to affordable financial services, regardless of their background or credit history. Our innovative approach combines traditional banking principles with modern technology to serve the unique needs of the Kenyan market.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our mission to transform lending in Kenya

2020

Foundation

FlexiFinance was founded with a vision to provide accessible credit solutions to underserved communities in Kenya.

2021

Digital Transformation

Launched our mobile-first platform, enabling customers to apply for loans and manage their accounts entirely through smartphones.

2022

Expansion & Growth

Reached 10,000 customers and disbursed over KES 500 million in loans, establishing ourselves as a trusted financial partner.

2023

Innovation & Recognition

Introduced AI-powered credit scoring and won "Best Fintech Innovation" award at the Kenya Digital Awards.

2024

Market Leadership

Achieved 50,000+ customers and KES 2 billion in total disbursements, becoming a leading player in Kenya's digital lending space.
