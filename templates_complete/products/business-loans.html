Business Loan Requirements

To qualify for a FlexiFinance business loan, your business must meet the following criteria:

Business Registration

Valid business registration certificate (KRA PIN)

Operating History

Minimum 12 months of business operations

Monthly Revenue

Minimum monthly revenue of KES 50,000

Bank Statements

6 months of business bank statements

Owner Details

Valid ID and proof of business ownership

Required Documents

Business Registration Certificate

KRA PIN certificate

Financial Statements

P&L statements (12 months)

Bank Statements

6 months business account

Business Plan

For loans over KES 200,000

Proof of Business Address

Utility bill or lease agreement
