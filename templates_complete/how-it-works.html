Eligibility Requirements

To qualify for a FlexiFinance loan, you must meet the following criteria:

Age

18 to 65 years old

Identity

Valid Kenyan National ID or passport

Employment

Stable employment or business income

Income

Minimum monthly income of KES 15,000

Banking

Active bank account for disbursement

Phone

Valid mobile number for verification

Required Documents

National ID or Passport

Clear, valid government-issued photo ID

Proof of Income

Recent pay slips or business records

Bank Statements

Last 3 months statements

Proof of Residence

Utility bill or tenancy agreement
