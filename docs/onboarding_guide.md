## KYC Verification Process

### What is KYC?
Know Your Customer (KYC) is a regulatory requirement to verify your identity before providing financial services.

### Required Documents

#### Primary Identification
- **Kenyan National ID:** Clear color photo of both sides
- **Passport:** Bio page (if no National ID)
- **Birth Certificate:** For applicants under 18 (with guardian)

#### Secondary Documents (If Required)
- **Bank Statement:** Last 3 months
- **Employment Letter:** From current employer
- **Business Registration:** For business loans
- **Pay Slip:** Recent salary slip

### Document Requirements

#### Photo Specifications

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### General Questions

**Q: What is the minimum loan amount?**
A: The minimum loan amount is KES 500 for emergency loans and KES 1,000 for regular loans.

**Q: How long does loan approval take?**
A: Emergency loans can be approved instantly, while regular loans take 24-48 hours.

**Q: Can I pay my loan early?**
A: Yes, early payments are encouraged and may reduce total interest paid.

**Q: What happens if I miss a payment?**
A: Late fees may apply, and your credit score may be affected. Contact us immediately if you're having trouble making payments.

**Q: How do I check my credit score?**
A: Your credit score is displayed on your dashboard and updated monthly.

### Account and Security

**Q: Can I change my registered phone number?**
A: Yes, contact support to update your phone number after verification.

**Q: Is my personal information secure?**
A: Yes, we use bank-level security and encryption to protect your data.

**Q: Can I have multiple active loans?**
A: Yes, you can have multiple loans as long as you meet the eligibility criteria.

**Q: How do I close my account?**
A: Contact support to initiate account closure after settling all outstanding loans.
