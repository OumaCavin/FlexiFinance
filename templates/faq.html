Can I apply for multiple loans at once?

You can have multiple active loans with FlexiFinance as long as you meet our eligibility criteria and can demonstrate the ability to repay multiple loans. Each application is assessed individually.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, and mobile money payments. You can also pay directly at any of our branches or partner locations across Kenya.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team 24/7 via phone at +254-708-101604, email at support@flexifinance.co.ke, or through our live chat feature on the website.

What if I don't have a credit history?

We understand that many Kenyans don't have traditional credit histories. We use alternative credit assessment methods including mobile money records, bank statements, and business performance to evaluate applications.

Can I repay my loan early?

Yes, you can repay your loan early without any prepayment penalties. Early repayment may actually improve your credit score and make you eligible for higher loan amounts in the future.
