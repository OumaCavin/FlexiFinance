Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can I get approved for a loan?
Our AI-powered system can provide instant decisions on most applications. Once approved, funds are typically disbursed within 24 hours via M-PESA or bank transfer.
What documents do I need to apply?
You'll need a valid Kenyan ID, proof of income (bank statements or salary slips), and proof of residence. Business loans may require additional business registration documents.
Can I repay my loan early?
Yes! You can repay your loan early without any penalty charges. This may even improve your credit score and qualify you for better rates on future loans.
How do I check my loan status?
Log into your FlexiFinance account to view real-time updates on your application status, loan balance, and payment schedule. You can also call our 24/7 support line.
What happens if I can't make a payment?
Contact us immediately if you're having trouble making a payment. We offer flexible repayment solutions and can work with you to avoid negatively impacting your credit score.
